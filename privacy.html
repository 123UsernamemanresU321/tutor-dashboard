Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 28 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, store, and protect personal information for students who use my tutoring dashboard (“the Dashboard”). By creating an account or using the Dashboard, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information I Collect

I collect only the minimum information necessary to run the tutoring service:

1.1 Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely via Supabase Authentication — I never see or store your password myself)

1.2 Academic Information

  • Subjects you are enrolled in
  • Session bookings and attendance
  • Homework/task status
  • Resources accessed (notes, worksheets, videos)

1.3 Billing Information

  • Outstanding invoices
  • Payment status (paid / overdue)
  • No credit card or banking details are stored on the Dashboard.

1.4 Automatically Stored Local Data

To protect your privacy, certain data (dashboard preferences, dark mode, cached resources) is stored only on your device using localStorage and IndexedDB. This information never leaves your device.

2. How the Information Is Used

  • To authenticate your account
  • To manage your tutoring sessions and schedule
  • To display your resources and homework
  • To track invoices and payments
  • To contact you regarding lessons, cancellations, or academic updates

I do not use your information for marketing or advertising. I do not share your data with any third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Supabase

Your email, name, and academic data are stored securely in Supabase. Supabase uses industry-standard security and encryption.

3.2 Local-Only Data

Anything stored in your browser (preferences, cached content) is local to your device, under your control, and deleted when you clear your browser data.

3.3 Access Control

Only I (the tutor) can view your academic-related data. I do not grant access to anyone else without your permission.

4. Data Sharing

Your data will never be sold, rented, or shared. The only time information might be disclosed is if legally required (e.g., a court order) or if you explicitly ask me to provide information to a parent or teacher. I do not share student data with schools unless you request it.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data, and to opt-out at any time. Requests can be made by contacting me directly.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as you remain a student. When you stop lessons, you may request deletion at any time.

7. Children and Minors

Because this service is offered within a school environment, many users are minors. I collect only essential information, do not use or share data for non-academic purposes, and follow parent or guardian instructions regarding account deletion.

8. Updates to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy if the Dashboard changes. If anything important changes, I will notify you.

9. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or want to request deletion of your data: erichuang.shangjing@outlook.com
